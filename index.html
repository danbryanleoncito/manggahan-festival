Manggahan Festival

is an annual cultural, agricultural, and food festival held in Guimaras, Philippines, primarily celebrating the province's world-renowned mangoes and its importance to the local economy. The festival also commemorates Guimaras becoming an independent province and is held every May. It's a major event that draws both locals and tourists.
